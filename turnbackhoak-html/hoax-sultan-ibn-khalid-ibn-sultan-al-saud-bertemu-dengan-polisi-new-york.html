[HOAX] Sultan ibn Khalid ibn Sultan Al-Saud Bertemu Dengan Polisi New York

SUMBER : MEDIA SOSIAL

NARASI :

WELCOME TO NEW YORK PRINCE AND MEET OUR POLICE

WHEN SAUDI PRINCE MEETS A NEW YORK COP

PENJELASAN :

1. Dia bukan Saudi prince.
2. Dia adalah Julienn Chabbott, pacar dari Stephanie Pratt (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stephanie_Pratt)
3. Khalid ibn Sultan Al-Saud tidak memiliki anak bernama Sultan.

“Khalid has three children from the first marriage to his first cousin Lulua, daughter of King Fahd which was terminated by divorce in 1978. Reena, the first born in this first marriage died within four months. The other two children are Faisal and Sara born in 1973 and 1976 respectively. He remarried Abeer bint Turki bin Abdulaziz with whom he has five children: Hala, Fahd, Salman, Mishail and Abdullah.” (http://houseofsaud.com/sa…/prince-khalid-bin-sultan-al-saud/)

4. Video kejadian tahun 2012, dan caption hoax muncul tahun 2013.
