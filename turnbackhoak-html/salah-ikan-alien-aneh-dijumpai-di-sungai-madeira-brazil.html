Hasil ciptaan animatronika disebut animatronik, yang antara lain berupa hewan (misalnya dinosaurus), tumbuhan, dan makhluk mitologis. Secara spesifik, animatronik yang dirancang sebagai imitasi manusia disebut android. Animatronika modern terutama diterapkan sebagai efek khusus film dan pada wahana di taman hiburan.

Animatronics is the use of robots to imitate humans or animals, or to make an inanimate object such as having the nature of living things. The results of animatronic creation are called animatronics, which include animals (eg dinosaurs), plants, and mythological creatures. Specifically, animatronics designed as human imitations are called android. Modern animatronics is mainly applied as a special effect of films and on rides in amusement parks.
