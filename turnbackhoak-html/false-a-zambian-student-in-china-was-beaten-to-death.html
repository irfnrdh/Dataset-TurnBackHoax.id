[FALSE] “A Zambian student in China was beaten to death”

The incident location of the shared video in the posts is in Malaysia, not China. It was a mob justice of a failed robbery attempt, check EXPLANATION and REFERENCE section for detail.

NARRATION

======

EXPLANATION

http://bit.ly/2qYG8Rs, First Draft News: “FALSE CONTEXT

When genuine content is shared with false contextual information”.

The correct context of the video is a mob justice incident which took place in Malaysia, not in China. Caused by a failed robbery attempt, not “dating a Chinese woman” as claimed by the circulating posts in SOURCE section,
